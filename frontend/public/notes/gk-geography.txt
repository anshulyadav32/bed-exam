GK Notes: Geography
===================

PHYSICAL GEOGRAPHY
- Layers of Earth: crust, mantle, outer core, inner core
- Types of rocks: igneous, sedimentary, metamorphic
- Landforms: mountains, plateaus, plains, valleys
- Climate zones: tropical, temperate, polar

INDIAN GEOGRAPHY
- Physiographic divisions: Himalayan region, Northern Plains, Peninsular Plateau, Coastal Plains, Islands
- Major rivers: Ganga, Yamuna, Brahmaputra, Godavari, Krishna, Cauvery
- Highest peak: Kangchenjunga (India); K2 (India-administered)
- States & capitals: all 28 states + 8 UTs

WORLD GEOGRAPHY
- Continents: 7 (Asia is largest; Australia is smallest)
- Oceans: 5 (Pacific is largest)
- Major countries, capitals, and currencies
- International organisations: UN, WHO, UNESCO headquarters

NATURAL RESOURCES
- Minerals: coal, iron ore, bauxite, mica — India's reserves
- Forest types: tropical evergreen, deciduous, thorn forests
- Soils: alluvial, black (regur), red, laterite

EXAM TIPS
- Practice map-based questions
- Know river → state → ocean drainage
- Wildlife sanctuaries & national parks frequently tested
