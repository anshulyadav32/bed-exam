GK Notes: Indian History
========================

ANCIENT HISTORY
- Indus Valley Civilization (2500-1750 BC): Harappa, Mohenjo-daro
- Vedic Period: Rigveda, caste system, Aryan settlement
- Maurya Empire: Chandragupta, Ashoka, spread of Buddhism
- Gupta Empire: Golden Age of India, science & art flourished

MEDIEVAL HISTORY
- Delhi Sultanate (1206-1526): Slave, Khilji, Tughlaq, Lodi dynasties
- Mughal Empire (1526-1857): Babur, Akbar, Aurangzeb
- Bhakti & Sufi movements: Kabir, Mirabai, Ramananda

MODERN HISTORY (BRITISH RULE & FREEDOM STRUGGLE)
- Battle of Plassey (1757): East India Company gains power
- 1857 Revolt: First War of Independence
- Indian National Congress (1885): political awakening
- Gandhi's movements: Non-Cooperation (1920), Civil Disobedience (1930), Quit India (1942)
- Independence: 15 August 1947

IMPORTANT PERSONALITIES
- Mahatma Gandhi: Father of the Nation
- Subhas Chandra Bose: Azad Hind Fauj
- Bhagat Singh, Chandrashekar Azad: revolutionary freedom fighters
- Dr. B. R. Ambedkar: architect of the Constitution

EXAM TIPS
- Focus on dates of major events
- Remember viceroys and their contributions
- Gandhi's movements timeline is frequently tested
